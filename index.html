As far as we know, the 65th Square Chess Club is the only chess club in the world with a bar. Here you can always find a partner for a game and enjoy a great cocktail named after the world's finest chess players.

Here's a selection of our classics:

Polgar

  • Tequila
  • Luxardo red bitter
  • Served on the rocks with a cherry and a slice of lime

Fischer

  • 2 ounces Malibu
  • 1/2 ounce orange liqueur or triple sec
  • 1/2 ounce lemon juice, freshly squeezed
  • 1 egg white

Capablanca

  • 2/3 glass full of ice
  • 2 ounces Brandy de Jerez
  • 1/4 ounce amaretto

Spassky

  • Vodka
  • White curacao or cointreau
  • Lime juice
  • Decorated with a mint cherry and sugar on a glass rim
